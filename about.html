Our Story

Founded in 2014, Dan Liu Dance Art School is one of the most famous dance schools in Dallas, Texas, USA. It provides professional dance and vocal education for children and adults, promotes and inherits Chinese culture, undertakes various large-scale performances and regularly participates in major competitions across the United States!

It has attracted much attention and favor for its scientific, rigorous and standardized professional dance teaching! Cultivate each student carefully and create a small class of high-quality classes to ensure that each class can win the proud result of "first place" in the overall score of platinum and double platinum in the National Dance Competition finals!

Our Mission

Our mission at Dan Liu Dance Art School is to Inspire Through Art, Nurture Through Beauty -- we provide students of all levels with more than just dance classes, but a unique experience in which the student can grow both mentally and physically as a dancer. Our highly trained and certified instructors have a wealth of experience in teaching and performing various styles and disciplines. Here at Dan Liu Dance Art School, we have a passion for the art of dance and for helping others discover their inner dancer.

Dan Liu – Founder and Principal

  • National Second Grade Actor
  • Member of Chinese Dancers Association
  • Former dance artist of Zhengzhou Railway Art Troupe
  • Professor of dance and vocal music at ZHENGZHOU NORMAL UNIVERSITY
  • Certified Instructor in the Grading and Examination System for Chinese Classical Dance and Ethnic/Folk Dance

In 1994, she visited Japan with the Henan delegation, and in 1997, she visited San Francisco, USA; in 2002, she visited ten European countries to perform condolences! In 2014, she immigrated to the United States and founded Liu Dan Dance Art School. She is famous for her scientific, rigorous and standardized professional dance teaching. Principal Liu Dan carefully cultivates each student and creates small and high-quality classes. All the classes she led have repeatedly won platinum and double platinum awards in the National Dance Competition finals.

In the 2019-2020 Global Youth Dance Finals, our founder and artistic director Ms. Liu Dan was honored with the prestigious Outstanding Choreographer Award. Our school was also recognized with the Best Dance Studio Award, a testament to our dedication to excellence in dance education. Most recently, at the 2024-2025 Pan-American International Taoli Cup Dance Competition – North America Finals, Ms. Liu Dan once again received the Outstanding Choreographer Award, and the school was awarded the distinguished title of "Home of Taoli Dancers", recognizing our continued leadership in cultivating young talent and promoting artistic innovation.
